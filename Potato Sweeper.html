How to play Potato Sweeper

There will be a 9x9 grid with 10 hidden potatoes. You will choose between 81 buttons
and choose between two radio buttons you can choose from, sweep or flag. Sweeping will check what is hidden underneath the coordinates.
If you hit a potato, you lose. If you hit a space and it shows you a number, that indicates how many number of potatoes is near the
coordinate you had chosen. This may be vertically, horizontally, or diagonally . If the square shows a + sign then there are no
potatoes near the coordinates. Flagging will not check what is underneath the grid and is used for indicating that the potato is at certain coordinates.
The objective is to flag all potatoes.
